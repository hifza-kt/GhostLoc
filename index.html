Privacy Policy for GhostLoc

Effective date: June 16, 2026

GhostLoc ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mock GPS and mapping utility designed for developer testing, QA workflows, and user-controlled location simulation. This Privacy Policy explains how GhostLoc handles sensitive user and device data.

By using GhostLoc, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

GhostLoc is designed to operate locally on your device. We do not require account registration and we do not operate our own server for storing your personal location data.

The app may process the following data on your device:

  • Selected map coordinates entered or chosen by the user.
  • Saved favorite locations and location history.
  • Route planner waypoints and route simulation settings.
  • Device location permission status.
  • Mock location service status.
  • Subscription status for GhostLoc Pro.

2. Location Data

GhostLoc requests location permissions so the app can:

  • Focus the map on your current device location when requested.
  • Support user-controlled mock location simulation.
  • Keep the foreground mock location service running while the user explicitly starts it.

GhostLoc does not continuously track your real location for advertising, analytics, profiling, or background monitoring. The mock location service runs only after the user starts it and displays a persistent foreground notification while active. The user can stop the service from the app or notification.

3. Mock Location and Developer Options

GhostLoc uses Android's mock location APIs for user-controlled testing purposes. To use mock location features, the user must manually select GhostLoc as the mock location app in Android Developer Options.

The app is intended for legitimate testing, development, and personal utility use. Users are responsible for complying with the terms of third-party apps, platforms, and services when using mock location features.

4. Local Storage

GhostLoc stores some data locally on your device, including:

  • Favorite locations.
  • Location history.
  • Route planner waypoints.
  • App settings.
  • Local premium access cache.

This data is stored locally and is not uploaded to a GhostLoc server. If you uninstall the app, local data such as favorites, history, and route planner entries may be deleted from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

GhostLoc may use third-party services to provide core app functionality, monetization, and map-related features.

Google Play Billing and RevenueCat

GhostLoc uses RevenueCat as a billing wrapper for Google Play Billing to manage subscriptions and restore purchases. RevenueCat and Google Play may process purchase-related information such as:

  • Product purchased.
  • Subscription status.
  • Transaction identifiers.
  • App user identifiers generated by the billing system.

GhostLoc uses this information only to determine whether GhostLoc Pro features should be unlocked.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob

GhostLoc may display ads to users who are not subscribed to GhostLoc Pro. AdMob may collect and process advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, and ad interaction data according to Google's policies.

GhostLoc Pro users do not see banner ads inside the app.

Google AdMob Privacy and Terms: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

OpenStreetMap / OSMDroid Map Tiles

GhostLoc uses OSMDroid and OpenStreetMap-based map tiles to display maps. When map tiles are loaded, map tile providers may receive network requests that can include your IP address and requested map tile area.

OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy: https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

Route Calculation Services

When the Route Planner feature is used, GhostLoc may request road route geometry from an external routing service, such as OSRM, to help draw direction lines and simulate movement along roads. This request may include the selected route waypoints. These waypoints are user-selected route points and are sent only when route calculation is requested.

OSRM Demo Server Usage Policy: https://github.com/Project-OSRM/osrm-backend/wiki/Api-usage-policy

6. Foreground Service and Notifications

GhostLoc uses a foreground location service when mock location simulation is active. This is required by Android so the service remains visible and controllable by the user.

The notification may show mock location activity status and route progress. The notification is persistent while mock location is active and can be removed after the mock location service is stopped.

7. Advertising and Premium Subscription

GhostLoc may offer both free and paid features:

  • Free users may see ads and may have feature limits.
  • GhostLoc Pro users unlock additional features such as route simulation, custom speed control, no ads, and longer usage limits.

Subscription payments are processed through Google Play and RevenueCat. GhostLoc does not directly collect or store payment card information.

8. Data Sharing

GhostLoc does not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with third-party providers only as necessary for:

  • Subscription management.
  • Ad display for free users.
  • Map tile loading.
  • Route calculation when requested by the user.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

9. Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until:

  • You delete it inside the app.
  • You clear app storage.
  • You uninstall the app.
  • The operating system removes app data.

Purchase and subscription records may be retained by Google Play and RevenueCat according to their own policies.

10. Security

GhostLoc uses reasonable technical measures to protect local app data and subscription status. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Users should avoid saving sensitive real-world locations if they do not want them stored locally on their device.

11. Children's Privacy

GhostLoc is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

12. User Choices

You can control app data and permissions by:

  • Granting or revoking location permissions in Android Settings.
  • Stopping mock location from the app or notification.
  • Deleting saved locations inside the app.
  • Clearing app data from Android Settings.
  • Uninstalling the app.
  • Managing or cancelling subscriptions through Google Play.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
